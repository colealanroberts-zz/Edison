Introduction

Edison is a beautiful, lightweight front end framework. Edison is built using Flexbox and its naming convention closely follows Yandex's BEM principles:

BEM – meaning block, element, modifier – is a front-end naming methodology thought up by the guys at Yandex. It is a smart way of naming your CSS classes to give them more transparency and meaning to other developers. They are far more strict and informative, which makes the BEM naming convention ideal for teams of developers on larger projects that might last a while. - source

This methodology makes Edison extremely modular while remaining lean and allows designers and developers to rapidly expand upon Edison's basic visual style.
